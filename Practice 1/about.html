Loveworld Singers

ABOUT

Loveworld Singers (The UK Band) was birthed by the Spirit of God under the leadership of the then LMAM UK Regional Coordinator, and Now General Manager of the LMAM International Secretariat, Pastor Soskie Omoregie and their first foray on the biggest stage in the world was at the very first International Music Concert with the Man of God in 2012, which took place in Lagos Nigeria!

Loveworld Records is an independent record label established by Chris Oyakhilome with the sole aim of discovering and developing gospel artists. Founded in 2009, the record label has gone on to groom and produce notable gospel musicians including Sinach, Frank Edwards, Joe Praize; among others. Some of the many artists include: Ada Ehi, Buchi, Chris Shalom, Eben, Frank Edwards, Jahdiel, Joe Praize, Sinach, Martin PK, Sophiya, cso, Tru South1, TB-1, Sam Jamz, Protek, Soltune, Winter, G Clan, Cyude, Mr Noble, Temple, Clinton Patrick, Lizzy abioye, and yomam.
